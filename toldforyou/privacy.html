Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 14, 2025  ·  Effective Date: May 14, 2025

Bairat Labs is committed to protecting children's privacy. We do not collect personal data from children without verifiable parental consent, we show no ads, and we never sell user data.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Bairat Labs (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Told for You mobile application (an AI-powered bedtime story app for children). By using Told for You, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We are committed to complying with applicable privacy laws including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimal information necessary to provide our services. Specifically, we may collect:

  • Account information via Google Sign-In: When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email address, display name, and profile picture. This is used solely to create and manage your account.
  • App usage data: Anonymised, non-personal usage statistics to help us improve the app (e.g., which features are used most). This data cannot be linked to individual users.
  • Device information: Basic device type and operating system version to ensure compatibility.

We do not collect:

  • Personal information directly from children under the age of 13
  • Location data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Contacts, photos, or media files
  • Biometric data of any kind

3. How We Use Information

Information we collect is used solely to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Provide and personalise the Told for You experience
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve and maintain our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for advertising and we do not create advertising profiles of any kind.

4. No Third-Party Advertising

Told for You contains no advertisements. We do not integrate any third-party advertising SDKs (such as Google AdMob, Facebook Audience Network, or any similar service). Your data is never used for targeted advertising by us or any third party.

5. No Sale of User Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any third party. Your data is not a product.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your account information is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use reputable cloud infrastructure providers and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or destruction.

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us (see Section 13).

7. COPPA Compliance — Children Under 13

Told for You is designed for children, used under the supervision of a parent or guardian. In accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.
  • Accounts are created and managed by parents or guardians on behalf of their children.
  • We do not condition a child's participation on the disclosure of more personal information than is reasonably necessary.
  • Parents have the right to review, modify, or request deletion of any personal information we hold about their child.
  • If we discover we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

To exercise parental rights, please contact us at bairatlabs@gmail.com with the subject line “Parental Rights Request”.

8. GDPR Compliance — European Users

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Legal basis: We process your data based on your consent (given when you create an account) and to fulfil our contractual obligation to provide the service.
  • Data minimisation: We collect only the data strictly necessary to provide Told for You.
  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Right to portability: You may request your data in a portable, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You may object to certain processing activities.
  • Data transfers: Where your data is transferred outside the EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at bairatlabs@gmail.com.

9. Parental Rights and Controls

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review the personal information we have collected
  • Request that we delete your child's personal information
  • Refuse to allow further collection or use of your child's information
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting the account

To exercise any of these rights, email us at bairatlabs@gmail.com with the subject line “Parental Rights Request — Told for You”.

10. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We may share data with trusted infrastructure providers (e.g., cloud hosting) who help us operate the app, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal process.
  • Safety: We may disclose information where necessary to protect the safety of our users or the public.

11. Cookies and Tracking

Told for You does not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking technologies. We may use minimal session-based storage solely to maintain your logged-in state within the app.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, through in-app notifications. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests relating to this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

Bairat Labs
Email: bairatlabs@gmail.com
Subject: “Privacy Inquiry — Told for You”

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 5 business days.
