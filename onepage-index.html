Six essentials, one calm place.

Everything both parents need to stay on the same page about the kids — without the back-and-forth across texts, spreadsheets, and email threads.

Custody calendar

Set a 2-2-3 rotation, week on/week off, every other weekend, or a fully custom schedule. Adjust individual days any time. Both parents always see the same view.

Co-parent messaging

Log what you paid, choose a split, and track who owes what. Both parents see the same running balance — no more disputed amounts or lost receipts.

OnePage Plus

More tools for families that need them.

OnePage Plus is an optional subscription that unlocks a handful of extra features. Everything above stays free, always.

AI tone check

Flags a message that might read as heated and offers a calmer rewrite before you send it. You always decide what goes through.

Expense approval flow

Log an expense and ask your co-parent to approve their share before it's added to the balance — no surprises, full transparency.

One-tap PDF export

Export a clean PDF summary of the custody schedule, expense ledger, and message log for any date range — for your own files or to share with a lawyer or mediator.

Support for more than one child

Track multiple children with individual profiles, allergies, school details, and their own expense records — all in one family account.
