Delete My Data

You're in control of your data. Here are the options available to you — from removing individual items to deleting your account entirely.

Remove specific items

You can delete individual messages, expenses, info-library entries, and photos directly inside the app. Deleted items are removed from Firestore and Firebase Storage immediately and are not recoverable.

  1. Open OnePage and navigate to the section containing the item.
  2. Long-press (or swipe left on iOS) to reveal the delete option.
  3. Confirm the deletion. The item is removed from both parents' views.
Permanent — cannot be undone

Delete your account

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, authentication credentials, and all data stored in OnePage — your schedule, messages, expenses, info-library entries, and photos. This cannot be undone.

If you are in an active co-parenting group: deleting your account will remove your contributions from the shared record. Your co-parent's account and any data they entered will not be affected.

  1. Open OnePage and go to Settings.
  2. Tap Account, then Delete Account.
  3. Follow the confirmation prompts. You may be asked to re-authenticate before deletion can proceed.
  4. Your data will be removed from our servers within 30 days.

Cancel OnePage Plus

Subscriptions are managed by the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) — not by OnePage directly. Cancel from the platform where you subscribed.

Platform How to cancel
iOS (App Store) Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → OnePage Plus → Cancel Subscription. Or visit apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.
Android (Google Play) Open Google Play → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → OnePage Plus → Cancel. Or visit play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions.

Cancelling your subscription stops future charges. You keep Plus access until the end of the current billing period. Cancelling a subscription does not delete your account or data.
