Support

Get help

Answers to the most common questions. If you don't find what you need, reach out and we'll get back to you.

How do I sign in?

Hanna uses Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google — there's no email and password option. Tap either option on the sign-in screen to get started.

If you're switching devices, sign in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used originally and your account will restore automatically.

Managing your subscription

iOS: Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Hanna → Cancel Subscription. Or visit apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

Android: Open Google Play → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Hanna → Cancel. Or visit play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions.

Cancelling stops future charges. You keep Premium access until the end of the billing period.

How does the free trial work?

When you subscribe to Premium, you get 7 days free. If you cancel within those 7 days you won't be charged. Trials are managed by the App Store or Google Play — we don't have access to billing information.

If you've had a trial before, Apple and Google may not offer a second one. Their policies apply.

Still need help?

Email us at bairatlabs@gmail.com — include "Hanna Support" in the subject line and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

You can also visit our contact page or read the privacy policy if you have questions about your data.

Important

Hanna is an AI companion, not a licensed therapist, and isn't a substitute for professional mental health care. If you're in crisis, please contact local emergency services or a crisis line in your area.

For immediate help in the US: 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988) · Crisis Text Line (text HOME to 741741). Find your local crisis line at findahelpline.com.
