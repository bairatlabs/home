PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026
The short version Growth Milestones is a fully offline app. It does not have a server, does not require an account, and does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. Everything you log — names, birthdates, measurements, photos, and notes — stays stored locally on your device.

1. Who we are

Growth Milestones is developed and published by Bairat Labs, LLC ("Bairat Labs," "we," "us"). This policy explains how the Growth Milestones mobile app handles information on iOS and Android.

2. No data collection

Growth Milestones does not have a backend server. The app makes no network requests to Bairat Labs, and we never receive, view, or store any of the following on any server we control:

  • Your child's name, birthdate, or gender
  • Height, weight, or other growth measurements
  • Milestone notes, dates, or custom entries
  • Photos you attach to a memory or child profile
  • Any account credentials — because no account is ever created

All of this information is generated by you, stored only on your device, and never leaves it unless you explicitly export or share it yourself (for example, by exporting a PDF memory book or saving a backup file).

3. How information is stored on your device

Children, milestones, and settings are saved in local on-device storage (such as AsyncStorage or an equivalent local database). Photos you attach are saved as local files in the app's private storage area on your phone or tablet. None of this data is synced to any cloud service, and Bairat Labs has no access to it.

4. Backup, export, and the memory book PDF

Growth Milestones lets you generate a few types of files entirely on your device:

  • JSON backup/export — a file containing your children and milestones, which you can save or transfer yourself (for example, to a new phone). This file is created and read locally; Bairat Labs never receives a copy.
  • Memory book PDF — a printable keepsake document built from your timeline, photos, and growth charts, generated on-device using your data.

Because these exports are created locally, what you do with the resulting file (where you save it, who you share it with) is entirely up to you. We recommend storing backups somewhere only you control.

5. Photos and camera/photo library access

If you attach a photo to a child's profile or a milestone, the app will ask for permission to access your photo library or camera. Selected photos are copied into the app's private local storage on your device. Photos are never uploaded anywhere by the app.

6. No third-party SDKs, ads, or analytics

Growth Milestones does not integrate any advertising network, analytics SDK, or crash-reporting service that transmits personal data off your device. There are no third-party trackers embedded in the app.

7. Optional premium features and RevenueCat

Growth Milestones may, now or in the future, offer an optional premium unlock (for example, to remove limits on PDF memory book exports). If and when this feature is enabled, purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store or Google Play, and purchase validation may be handled using RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service.

  • RevenueCat only ever sees purchase/entitlement information needed to validate your purchase (such as an anonymous device or purchase identifier) — never your child's name, photos, or milestone data, which always stay local to your device.
  • Apple and Google handle all payment details directly; Bairat Labs never sees or stores your card information.
  • You can review RevenueCat's own privacy practices at revenuecat.com/privacy.

8. Children's privacy (COPPA)

Growth Milestones is a tool used by parents and caregivers to record information about their own children. Because all of that information is stored exclusively on the parent's own device and is never transmitted to or collected by Bairat Labs, the app does not collect personal information from children within the meaning of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, child or adult, on our servers — because we do not operate any server that the app communicates with for this purpose.

9. Notifications

If Growth Milestones offers local reminders (for example, to log a new measurement), these are scheduled and delivered entirely on-device through the operating system's notification system. They do not involve any push notification server operated by us.

10. Third-party services

Aside from the optional RevenueCat purchase validation described above (section 7), the app primarily relies on standard operating system services, such as:

ServicePurpose
Apple App Store / Google PlayApp distribution and (if applicable) in-app purchases
Device photo library / cameraLetting you attach a photo to a memory, stored locally
RevenueCat (optional)Validating optional premium purchases, if and when offered

These providers' own privacy policies govern any information collected at the platform level (such as your App Store account), which is outside of our control.

11. Data retention and deletion

Since all data lives on your device, you are always in control of it. See our data deletion page for step-by-step instructions on clearing your data or removing the app entirely.

12. Security

Because your data never leaves your device, the main safeguard is your device's own security (passcode, biometric lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date and using a passcode or biometric lock to protect access to the app.

13. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised policy at this same address.

14. Contact us

Questions about this policy or how Growth Milestones handles information? Reach us at bairatlabs@gmail.com or on Reddit at u/bairatlabs.
