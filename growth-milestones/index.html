FEATURES

Everything a baby book should be

Simple, warm tools for capturing the moments that matter — built to last as long as the memories do.

📏

Height & weight tracking

Log measurements over time and watch a smooth growth chart take shape, in cm/in or kg/lb.

💬

Firsts, captured

First words, first steps, first day of school — or create a custom milestone for anything in between.

🖼️

Photos & notes

Attach a photo and a note to any memory. Everything is stored privately, right on your device.

📈

Growth charts

Watch height and weight trends over time with gentle, tappable charts and at-a-glance stats.

📖

Printable memory book

Export a beautifully laid out PDF keepsake book — a cover, timeline pages, growth charts, and more.

👨‍👩‍👧

Multiple children

Keep a separate story for each child in your family, and switch between them in a tap.
