Deleting your data

Growth Milestones stores everything locally on your device — there is no Bairat Labs account or server copy to delete. Here is exactly how to remove your data, depending on what you want to clear.

Option 1

Delete a single child or memory, in-app

  1. Open Growth Milestones and go to that child's Timeline or Settings → Children.
  2. To remove one memory: open it and tap Delete at the bottom of the milestone detail screen.
  3. To remove a child's entire profile (including their milestones and photos): go to Edit profile for that child and tap Remove child, then confirm.
This permanently deletes the selected memory or child profile, including any attached photos, from your device. This action cannot be undone, so consider exporting a backup first if you might want the data later.
Option 2

Clear all app data, in-app

  1. Open Settings within Growth Milestones.
  2. Look under Back up & export data for a "Clear all data" or "Reset app" option.
  3. Confirm the reset. This removes every child, milestone, note, and photo stored by the app.

If you'd like to keep a copy first, use the same screen to export a JSON backup or generate a memory book PDF before clearing your data.

Option 3

Uninstall the app

Because Growth Milestones keeps all of its data in local app storage on your device, uninstalling the app removes everything it stored, including:

  • All children's profiles and milestone entries
  • All photos attached within the app
  • Any local backups saved inside the app's own storage area
PlatformHow to uninstall
iOSTouch and hold the Growth Milestones icon on your Home Screen, then tap "Remove App" → "Delete App."
AndroidTouch and hold the Growth Milestones icon, then tap "Uninstall" (or go to Settings → Apps → Growth Milestones → Uninstall).
If you exported a JSON backup or PDF memory book to your device's Files app, Photos, or Downloads, those exported files are separate from the app and will not be removed when you uninstall — delete them separately if you want them gone too.
About subscriptions

Cancelling an optional premium purchase

If you've purchased an optional premium unlock, that subscription or one-time purchase is managed by the App Store or Google Play, not by Bairat Labs. Deleting app data or uninstalling the app does not cancel a subscription.

  • iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Growth Milestones → Cancel Subscription.
  • Android: Google Play app → Menu → Subscriptions → Growth Milestones → Cancel.

Still need help?

Since Bairat Labs has no access to your on-device data, we cannot delete it remotely on your behalf — but we're happy to help if you run into trouble with any of the steps above.
