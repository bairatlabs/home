Made for families

A tiny ritual. A lasting habit.

🪲
Meet Glowy
A friendly firefly whose belly glows brighter with each answer. No streaks, no points — just warmth that grows one noticed night at a time.
🎤
Just say it
Pre-readers can hold the mic and speak out loud. Voice-to-text uses your phone's own on-device speech — no recordings are ever kept or sent anywhere.
📅
Glowing calendar
Every noticed night lights up golden. Empty nights are just resting — never a failure. Tap any past night to revisit what your child said.
📄
Monthly keepsake PDF
At month's end, export a beautifully formatted PDF with every glowing night. Print it, frame it, tuck it in a drawer to treasure for years.
👨‍👩‍👦
Multiple kids
Add several children on one device. Each child gets their own colour, their own Glowy, their own calendar — managed in the parent zone.
🔔
Gentle bedtime reminder
Set a soft nightly notification so Glowy nudges the family when it's time for three things. Easy to toggle off anytime in the parent zone.

Private by design

We collect nothing. We mean it.

Glowy is built from the ground up to be safe for children and honest for parents. Your child's words stay exactly where they belong — on your device.

🔒
Stays on your deviceNo account, no login, no cloud. Every entry lives only on your phone and is never uploaded.
🚫
No ads or trackingNo analytics. No third-party SDKs. We built Glowy to be used, not to collect data from your family.
🎤
On-device voice onlyVoice-to-text uses your phone's own speech recognition — no audio is ever recorded or sent anywhere.
🔐
PIN-protected parent zoneBack up data, manage children's profiles, or delete everything — all behind a parent-only PIN.
