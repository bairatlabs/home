Delete your Glowy data

This page explains how to remove your data from Glowy. Because Glowy stores everything locally on your device — with no servers and no accounts — you are fully in control of deletion at any time.

Important: Glowy is a fully offline app. We at Bairat Labs hold no data about you or your child on our servers. All deletion happens on your device, instantly.

Option 1 — Delete everything inside the app

Using the parent zone

  1. Open Glowy and tap parent zone at the bottom of the main screen.
  2. Enter your parent PIN to unlock the zone.
  3. Tap "delete everything" (shown in red at the bottom of the settings list).
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

This immediately removes all children's profiles, all journal entries, all calendar history, and all app settings from your device. The action cannot be undone — we recommend using "back up data" first if you want to keep a copy.

Option 2 — Uninstall the app

Remove Glowy from your device

Uninstalling Glowy from your device removes all locally stored data automatically:

  • iOS: Press and hold the Glowy icon → tap "Remove App" → "Delete App".
  • Android: Press and hold the Glowy icon → tap "Uninstall".

This deletes all app data at the OS level. No data remains on your device or anywhere else after uninstallation.
