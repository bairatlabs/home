Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Bairat Labs, LLC ("Bairat Labs", "we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Glowy app ("Glowy", "the App"). Please read it carefully.

The short version: we collect nothing.

Glowy is designed so that all data — your child's journal entries, voice inputs, calendar history, and profile — stays entirely on your device. There are no accounts, no logins, and nothing is ever uploaded to our servers or any third-party service. We do not run servers that hold your data because we built Glowy so none is needed.

1. Who we are

Bairat Labs, LLC is the developer of Glowy. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at bairatlabs@gmail.com.

2. Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect, transmit, store, or have access to any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your child's name, age, or any information about your child
  • Journal entries, gratitude responses, or any content your child types or speaks
  • Voice recordings or audio from the microphone
  • Calendar data or usage history
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioural telemetry
  • Any payment or billing information

We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs (such as Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar), advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting tools that would transmit data off your device.

3. What stays on your device

All data created in Glowy is stored locally on your device using your operating system's standard on-device storage. This includes:

  • Parent and child profiles (names, colours, age settings)
  • Journal entries for every night — grateful, learned, and tomorrow responses
  • Sticker choices and doodles
  • Calendar history and glowing night records
  • The parent zone PIN
  • App settings including bedtime reminders

None of this data leaves your device unless you choose to share it (for example, by exporting the monthly keepsake PDF and sharing it yourself).

4. Voice and microphone use

Glowy offers a "say it" feature that lets children speak their answers aloud instead of typing. This feature uses your device's built-in, on-device speech recognition provided by iOS (Apple's Speech framework) or Android (Google's on-device speech engine).

We do not record, store, or transmit any audio. No voice data is sent to Bairat Labs' servers — because we have no servers receiving it. Speech processing happens entirely on your device according to Apple's or Google's own privacy policies for their system-level speech APIs.

You can use Glowy without ever enabling the microphone — typing is always available as an alternative.

5. Keepsake PDF export

The "make this month's keepsake" feature generates a PDF document on your device containing your child's journal entries for the month. This PDF is created locally and is never sent to us. You choose what to do with it — save it to your device, print it, or share it. We have no visibility into whether you export a PDF or what it contains.

6. Parent zone

The parent zone is protected by a PIN that you set on first use. The PIN is stored only on your device. We do not have access to your PIN and cannot reset it on your behalf. The parent zone allows you to:

  • Add or edit children's profiles
  • Back up your data as a local JSON file (saved to your device only)
  • Delete all profiles and entries from the app
  • Configure bedtime reminders

7. Children's privacy (COPPA and similar laws)

Glowy is designed for use by children aged 5–11 under parental supervision. Because we collect no personal information whatsoever from any user — child or adult — Glowy is inherently compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar children's privacy laws in other jurisdictions: there is simply no data to collect, share, or protect on our end.

We strongly encourage parents to review the app's content with their children and to use the parent zone to manage how the app is used.

8. Notifications

If you enable the optional bedtime reminder, Glowy will request permission to send local push notifications. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device — no notification data is sent to our servers. You can disable notifications at any time in the app or in your device's system Settings.

9. Third-party services

Glowy does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics services, social media SDKs, or data brokers. The only third-party technology involved is:

  • Apple's Speech framework / Google on-device speech — for the optional voice input feature, operating according to the respective platform's privacy policies.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — for app distribution. These platforms may collect certain purchase and download data according to their own privacy policies when you install or purchase the app.

10. Data retention and deletion

Because all data lives on your device, you are in full control of its retention and deletion.

  • To delete all data: Open the parent zone → tap "delete everything". This removes all profiles, journal entries, and app data from the device immediately.
  • To delete the app and all data: Uninstall Glowy from your device in the normal way. All locally stored data is removed when the app is uninstalled.

Since we hold no data on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete on our end.

11. Security

All data is stored using your device operating system's standard local storage mechanisms, which benefit from device-level encryption (when you have a device passcode set). The parent zone adds an additional in-app PIN layer for sensitive settings. We recommend keeping your device up to date and using a device passcode for maximum protection.

12. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will also include a notice in the app. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
