About Bairat Labs

Bairat Labs, LLC is a Delaware-registered technology company building AI-native consumer products. Our first product is Told for You, an AI-powered personalised bedtime story application for children aged 2 to 12.

Our Mission

To use artificial intelligence to create deeply personal, meaningful moments between parents and children — starting with bedtime.

Told for You

Told for You generates a unique, narrated bedtime story for a child every single night. Parents enter their child's name, age, and a few favourite things. The app creates a fully personalised story in seconds, narrated aloud in a warm, natural voice. Every story ends with the child's name. No two stories are ever the same.

The product is built entirely on Google's infrastructure — Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite for story generation and Google Cloud Neural2 Text-to-Speech for narration — with Firebase powering authentication, real-time database, and cloud storage. The app is built in React Native with Expo and is currently in closed testing on Google Play, with an Apple App Store submission pending.
