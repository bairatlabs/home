Privacy Policy

Bairat Labs, LLC  ·  Effective July 6, 2026  ·  App: Hanna

The short version

Hanna is an AI wellness companion. To work, it needs to store your account details and the content of your conversations in the cloud. We collect only what the app needs, we don't sell your data, we don't show you ads, and we don't share your information with anyone except the services listed in this policy that help us run the app.

1. Who we are

Hanna is made by Bairat Labs, LLC, a software company based in the United States. If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at bairatlabs@gmail.com.

Registered business address: 131 Continental Dr, Suite 305, Newark, DE 19713, United States

2. What we collect and why

We collect only what the app needs to function. All data listed below is linked to your user account. None of this data is used for third-party advertising or ad tracking.

Data type What it includes Why we collect it
Name & Email Address Provided when you sign in with Apple or Google Account identity and authentication
User ID A unique identifier assigned to your account Links your data across sessions and devices

3. How we use your data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Hanna app. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you — sign-in via Apple or Google, and maintain your session
  • To power conversations — store and retrieve conversation history so Hanna can remember context
  • To enable mood tracking — store and analyse your check-in history so you can see patterns over time
  • To manage subscriptions — verify Premium entitlement and process purchases through the App Store or Google Play via RevenueCat
  • To improve the product — aggregate, anonymised analytics help us understand how features are used so we can make Hanna better

We do not use your data for third-party advertising, targeted marketing, or ad tracking of any kind. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their commercial purposes.

4. Infrastructure & third-party services

We use the following services to operate Hanna. Each processes data only as necessary to provide their function:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — We use Firebase Authentication (for sign-in) and Cloud Firestore (for data storage including conversations, mood data, and user settings). Firebase is operated by Google LLC and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare Workers (Cloudflare, Inc.) — Our backend API runs on Cloudflare Workers, which proxies requests between the app and our AI infrastructure. Cloudflare processes data in transit and is subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat, Inc. — Manages subscription purchases, entitlements, and receipt validation. RevenueCat processes purchase receipts from the App Store and Google Play. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Inc. / Google LLC — Sign in with Apple and Sign in with Google handle authentication; App Store and Google Play process subscription purchases. Their respective privacy policies apply to those interactions.
  • Anthropic, PBC (Claude API) — Claude powers AI conversation in Hanna. Your messages are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Anthropic does not use API data to train its models. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Google LLC (Gemini API) — Gemini is used for AI conversation processing in certain contexts. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to allow you to restore your account if you change your mind.

If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer. Aggregated, anonymised analytics data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained indefinitely.

To delete your account and data, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account inside the Hanna app, or contact us and we'll handle your request within 30 days.

6. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under applicable privacy law (including GDPR and CCPA) that include:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your personal data (see Section 5 above)
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection / restriction — object to or ask us to restrict certain types of processing
  • Opt out of sale — we do not sell personal data; this right is not applicable, but you may exercise it by contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, email us at bairatlabs@gmail.com with the subject line "Hanna Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days.

If you are in the European Economic Area and believe we are handling your data in violation of GDPR, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Children's privacy

Hanna is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in jurisdictions where that higher age applies). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children below these ages.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at bairatlabs@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest via Firebase's built-in encryption.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you have concerns about the security of your data, please contact us.

9. International data transfers

Bairat Labs is based in the United States. If you use Hanna from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country.

Where required (for example, for users in the European Economic Area), we rely on appropriate transfer mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses. Governing law: the State of Delaware, United States.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Effective" date at the top and, for material changes, provide in-app notice or email you at the address associated with your account.

Continued use of Hanna after the updated policy becomes effective constitutes your acceptance of the changes.
