Delete Your Hanna Account

You can request deletion of your Hanna account and all associated personal data at any time. Choose whichever method is easiest for you.

Delete in the app Fastest

Follow these steps inside the Hanna app:

  • Open Hanna and tap the "You" tab at the bottom of the screen
  • Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account [TODO: confirm exact in-app menu path]
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted

What gets deleted

Data permanently deleted

  • Account profile — your name, email address, and account credentials
  • Chat & conversation history — all text and voice conversations with Hanna
  • Mood check-in data — all logged moods, notes, and check-in history
  • Voice recordings — any audio data tied to your account from voice mode sessions
What may be retained

Data that may be kept after deletion [TODO: confirm retention specifics]

  • Anonymised usage analytics — aggregate data that cannot be linked back to you (e.g. total sessions, feature usage counts). This contains no personal information.
  • Transaction records — purchase and subscription receipts retained by RevenueCat and the App Store / Google Play for legal and billing compliance. These records are held by the payment processors under their own retention policies.
Important: subscriptions

Deleting your account does not cancel your subscription. If you have an active Hanna Premium subscription, you must cancel it separately through the App Store or Google Play before or after deleting your account — otherwise you may continue to be billed.

To cancel on iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions
To cancel on Android: Google Play → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
