Privacy Policy

Bairat Labs, LLC·Effective July 5, 2026·App: OnePage

The short version: OnePage is a co-parenting coordination tool. To work, it needs to store your schedule, messages, and expenses in the cloud so both parents see the same information. We store only what the app needs, we don't sell your data, we don't show you ads, and we don't share your information with anyone except the services listed in this policy that help us run the app.

1. Who we are

OnePage is made by Bairat Labs, LLC, a software company based in the United States. If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at bairatlabs@gmail.com.

2. What we collect and why

OnePage collects only the information it needs to provide the service. Here is what each category covers and why it's needed:

Category What it includes Why it's needed
Account Email address, display name, profile photo (optional), authentication tokens To identify your account and let you sign in
Custody schedule Rotation pattern, individual day overrides, handoff times and locations To show both parents the same calendar view
Messages Text of messages sent between co-parents, timestamps, read receipts To deliver messages and maintain the permanent, uneditable record
Expenses Description, amount, date, payee, split ratio, approval status, optional receipt photo To track the shared expense ledger and show the running balance
Info library Child profiles: name, birthday, doctor, allergies, school, emergency contacts, pickup authorization, other notes To give both parents access to essential child information
Photos and videos Files you upload to the shared album To power the shared family media library
Subscription Purchase token, subscription status, store ID (App Store or Google Play) To validate your OnePage Plus subscription and unlock Plus features

We do not collect device identifiers for advertising, browsing history, location data, or any information beyond what is listed above.

3. How we store your data

OnePage stores data using Google Firebase, a cloud platform operated by Google LLC. Firebase services we use include:

  • Firebase Authentication — manages your account and sign-in. Supports email/password, Sign in with Apple, and Sign in with Google.
  • Cloud Firestore — stores structured data: your schedule, messages, expenses, and info-library entries.
  • Firebase Storage — stores files you upload: photos, videos, and receipt images.

Firebase data is stored in Google-operated data centers. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Firebase's own privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Access to your family's data is restricted to the accounts that belong to your co-parenting group. Other OnePage users cannot see your information.

4. Sign-in methods

You can create a OnePage account using:

  • Email and password — stored and managed by Firebase Authentication.
  • Sign in with Apple — we receive a unique Apple ID token. Apple may provide a relay email address; we store only the token and name Apple provides. Apple's privacy practices are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Sign in with Google — we receive a Google ID token and your public Google profile name and photo (if provided). Google's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not receive your Apple or Google password. Authentication is handled entirely by Apple and Google respectively, using OAuth 2.0.

5. AI tone check (OnePage Plus)

OnePage Plus subscribers can use the optional AI tone check feature before sending a message. When you tap "Check tone," the current text of your draft is sent to our Cloudflare Worker proxy, which forwards it to Anthropic's Claude API to analyze the tone and suggest a calmer rewrite if appropriate.

Important: what happens to your message draft

Your draft text is transmitted to Anthropic's API solely to generate the tone-check result. It is not logged by OnePage, not stored in your account, and not used to train AI models. The draft is discarded after the API call completes. The message you actually send (after any edits) is stored normally in Firestore as described in Section 3.

The tone-check feature is entirely optional and runs only when you explicitly tap the "Check tone" button. Nothing is sent to the AI without your action. Anthropic's data practices for API calls are governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

The Cloudflare Worker proxy handles routing and authentication for the AI request. Cloudflare's data practices are governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

6. Subscriptions

OnePage Plus subscriptions are managed through RevenueCat, a subscription management platform. When you subscribe, RevenueCat receives your purchase token from the App Store or Google Play and validates your subscription status.

RevenueCat stores your subscription state (active, expired, or cancelled) and associates it with your account. RevenueCat does not receive your payment card details — those are handled entirely by Apple or Google. RevenueCat's privacy practices are governed by the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

7. How we share your data

We share your data only as described in this policy:

  • With your co-parent — data you put in OnePage (schedule, messages, expenses, info library, shared photos) is shared with the other accounts in your co-parenting group by design. That is the core function of the app.
  • With Firebase / Google — to store and sync your data (see Section 3).
  • With Anthropic (tone check only) — your message draft text is sent only when you invoke the tone-check feature (see Section 5).
  • With RevenueCat — subscription status for OnePage Plus (see Section 6).
  • With Cloudflare — as the routing proxy for the tone-check API call (see Section 5).

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use your data or your children's data for any purpose other than operating the app.

8. Children's information

OnePage is designed for use by parents and guardians, not by children. Users of the app must be 13 or older (or the age of digital consent in their country). We do not knowingly allow accounts to be created by minors.

The app's info-library feature allows parents to store information about their children (medical records, school contacts, allergies, etc.). This data is stored in Firestore as described in Section 3, is visible only to the co-parenting group, and is used solely to help parents coordinate their children's care. We do not share children's information with third parties for advertising or any other purpose.

If you believe a minor has created an account or that children's data has been submitted in error, please contact us at bairatlabs@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your rights and choices

You can exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Access — your data is visible within the app at all times.
  • Correction — you can edit your info-library entries, schedule, and profile from within the app.
  • Deletion — you can delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Account → Delete Account. Data is purged from Firestore and Firebase Storage within 30 days of the request. See our Delete My Data page for full instructions.
  • Export — OnePage Plus subscribers can export a PDF summary of their data at any time using the Export feature in the app.
  • Opt out of tone check — simply don't tap "Check tone." The feature is never invoked automatically.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the UK, or another jurisdiction with additional privacy rights (such as GDPR or CCPA), you may contact us to exercise those rights. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is removed from Firestore and Firebase Storage within 30 days. Firebase Authentication credentials are deleted within 7 days of the account-deletion request.

Subscription records held by RevenueCat may be retained for up to 7 years as required by financial record-keeping obligations; this is limited to the purchase token and subscription status and contains no personally identifiable payment information.

11. Security

All data transmitted between the app and our backend is encrypted in transit using TLS. Data stored in Firestore and Firebase Storage is encrypted at rest by Google. Access to your family's data is enforced by Firebase Security Rules, which restrict read and write access to authenticated members of your co-parenting group.

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in OnePage, please report it to bairatlabs@gmail.com.

12. Analytics and advertising

OnePage does not include third-party advertising SDKs. We do not display ads.

We may use Firebase Analytics (which is part of Google Firebase) to collect aggregate, anonymized usage statistics — for example, how often certain features are used. This data is used only to improve the app and is not linked to your account or shared with advertisers. You can opt out of Firebase Analytics data collection in Settings on your device.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you within the app and update the "Effective" date at the top of this page. Continued use of OnePage after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
