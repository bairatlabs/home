Delete your Zuppy account & data

This page explains how to request deletion of your Zuppy account and the data associated with it. Zuppy is published by Bairat Labs, LLC.

How to request deletion

You have two ways to request that your Zuppy account and your household’s data be deleted. Both result in the same outcome — pick whichever is easier.

Option 1 — Request by email (recommended)

  1. Send an email to bairatlabs@gmail.com with the subject line “Account Deletion Request”.
  2. Include the following in the body so we can locate your household:
    • The email address or Apple ID you sign in with
    • Your house code (looks like ZUP-XXXX, visible on the Family screen in the app)
    • Your household name (e.g. “The Smiths”)
  3. We will confirm receipt within 2 business days and complete the deletion within 7 days.

Email a deletion request

Option 2 — Delete in the app

  1. Open Zuppy and sign in as the primary parent.
  2. Open More → Settings.
  3. Scroll to the Danger Zone at the bottom.
  4. Tap “Request account & data deletion” — this opens a pre-filled email to our support address. Send it to complete the request.

In-app deletion immediately signs you out of the device. Server-side data is purged within 7 days as described below.

What data is deleted

When your deletion request is processed, we permanently remove the following from our active systems:

DataWhat it is
Account profileYour name, email address, Apple sign-in identifier, profile photo (if any), and authentication credentials.
HouseholdThe household record including all member profiles (parents and children), house code, and household settings.
Chores & activityAll chore assignments, submissions, photo proofs, parent reviews, and chore history.

GamificationStreaks, badges, statistics, and chore swaps.

Subscription statePro entitlement records associated with your household. (Note: this does not cancel an active Apple or Google Play subscription — see below.)
ReferralsOutbound and inbound referral records linked to your household.

What data may be retained

A small amount of information may be retained for a limited period after deletion, strictly to meet legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention obligations:

DataWhy retainedHow long
Encrypted backupsAutomatic snapshots of our database used for disaster recovery.Up to 30 days, after which backups are overwritten and deleted.
Anonymized usage logsApplication logs with no personally identifying information, used to diagnose crashes and improve reliability.Up to 90 days.
Transaction recordsRecords of any in-app purchases made through the Apple App Store or Google Play (e.g. Zuppy Pro subscription), kept for tax and accounting purposes. We do not store your payment card; Apple and Google handle that.As required by applicable tax law (typically 7 years).

Cancelling an active subscription

Deleting your Zuppy account does not automatically cancel an active Zuppy Pro subscription — subscriptions are managed by the App Store or Google Play, not by us.

To cancel your subscription before requesting deletion:

  • iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Zuppy → Cancel Subscription.
  • Android: Play Store app → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Zuppy → Cancel subscription.
