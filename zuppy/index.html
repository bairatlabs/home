The Spend • Save • Give Wallet

Three jars. Lifelong lessons.

Every dollar kids earn is automatically split into three jars — teaching real money habits that stick long after childhood.

🛍

Spend

Guilt-free spending for things kids want now. Seeing the balance go down teaches real budgeting instincts.

🃏

Save

Set savings goals for big-ticket items. Watching progress toward something they want builds patience.

❤️

Give

Choose a cause, donate some earnings. Kids develop empathy and generosity as a natural part of earning.
