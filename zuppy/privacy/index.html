Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 14, 2025  ·  Effective Date: May 14, 2025

Bairat Labs is committed to protecting children’s privacy. We do not collect personal data from children without verifiable parental consent, we show no ads, and we never sell user data.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Bairat Labs (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Zuppy mobile application. By using Zuppy, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union and United Kingdom.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Sign in with Apple: When you sign in with Apple, we receive only the information Apple shares with us — this may include a name and an email address (which Apple may relay or anonymise). We use this solely to create and manage your account. We do not receive your Apple ID password.
  • Sign in with Google: When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email address, display name, and profile picture. This is used solely to create and manage your account.
  • App usage data: Anonymised, non-personal usage statistics to help us improve the app. This data cannot be linked to individual users.
  • Device information: Basic device type and operating system version to ensure compatibility.

We do not collect: personal data directly from children under 13, location data, financial or payment information, contacts, photos, media files, or biometric data of any kind.

3. How We Use Information

Information we collect is used solely to create and maintain your account, provide and personalise the Zuppy experience, respond to support requests, improve and maintain our services, and comply with legal obligations. We do not use your information for advertising and we do not create advertising profiles.

4. No Third-Party Advertising

Zuppy contains no advertisements. We do not integrate any third-party advertising SDKs (such as Google AdMob or Facebook Audience Network). Your data is never used for targeted advertising.

5. No Sale of User Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any third party. Your data is not a product.

6. Data Storage and Security

Account information is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use reputable cloud infrastructure providers and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data. We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. COPPA Compliance — Children Under 13

Zuppy is designed for children, used under the supervision of a parent or guardian. In accordance with COPPA:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.
  • Accounts are created and managed by parents or guardians on behalf of their children.
  • We do not condition a child’s participation on providing more personal information than is reasonably necessary.
  • Parents have the right to review, modify, or request deletion of any personal information we hold about their child.
  • If we inadvertently collect personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it promptly.

To exercise parental rights: bairatlabs@gmail.com — Subject: “Parental Rights Request”

8. GDPR Compliance — European Users

  • Legal basis: Consent and contractual necessity.
  • Data minimisation: We collect only what is strictly necessary to provide Zuppy.
  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to portability: You may request your data in a portable, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You may object to certain processing activities.
  • Data transfers: Where data is transferred outside the EEA, appropriate safeguards are in place.

To exercise any of these rights: bairatlabs@gmail.com

9. Parental Rights and Controls

As a parent or guardian you may review, correct, or delete your child’s personal information, and withdraw consent at any time by deleting the account. Email bairatlabs@gmail.com — Subject: “Parental Rights Request — Zuppy”.

10. Data Sharing

We share data only with trusted infrastructure providers under strict confidentiality agreements, or where required by law, or to protect the safety of our users or the public.

11. Cookies and Tracking

Zuppy does not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking technologies. Minimal session-based storage is used solely to maintain your logged-in state within the app.

12. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, through in-app notifications.
